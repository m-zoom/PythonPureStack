How It Works
  1. Upload a photo or capture one using your webcam
  2. The system will detect faces in the image
  3. Faces are compared against all registered users
  4. Matches are returned with a confidence score
  5. Adjust the threshold to control match sensitivity
For Best Results
  • Use clear, well-lit images
  • Face should be clearly visible and not obscured
  • Image should be front-facing with neutral expression
